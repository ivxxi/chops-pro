Beef

Chops Pro supplies the finest quality, tender and pink Whole beef and veal sourced from our renowned Laikipia County. Here the calves and cattles are raised exclusively for meat production.

Kenyan is reputed for production of grass-fed Boran Cattles. We have over the years specialized in production and supply of Boran Cattles of premium or high grade.

Our supply chain developed by Chops Pro gives us access to best grass-fed livestock to supply the various market segments with the finest beef all year around.

Lamb

Chops Pro specializes in the supply of Whole Chilled or Frozen Kenyan Lamb for markets throughout Middle East, Kenya and Africa.

By working closely with producers we maintain the reliability and integrity of our supply chain in order to meet the high expectations of our customers.

Our popular Kenyan lamb/sheep varieties are; The Black Head Sheep, Dorper Sheep and Merino sheep.

We specialize in only grass-fed goat. Our carcass weight range is 8-15 Kgs

Goat

Chops Pro also specializes in the supply of Whole Chilled or Frozen Kenyan Goat for markets throughout Middle East, Kenya and Africa.

Our Kenyan Goat is widely renowned and loved across the Middle East market for being tender and delicious. We serve UAE, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar and Saudi Arabia with our high quality goat which is unmatched across the region.

Chops Pro Limited is a specialist exporter of premium Halal Kenyan Goat, Lamb, Sheep, Beef and Veal in Chilled and Frozen form.
